Gramformer A framework for detecting and correcting grammatical errors on natural language text (NLP)
Gramformer is a library that exposes 3 seperate interfaces to a family of algorithms to detect, highlight and correct grammar errors.
To make sure the corrections and highlights recommended are of high quality, it comes with a quality estimator.
